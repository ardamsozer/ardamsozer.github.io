    Benefits

    — Benefits of Wearing Custom-Fitting Protective Gear

    Both Post-Surgical and Preventative Benefits for All

    Custom-fitting orthotics does not only help those who have had injuries return to their physical activities quicker, but are also highly effective as preventative measures. A broken nose or a dislocated wrist will never be as good as it once was, and using protective gear will not only save face but also money long-term.

    If you've recently had nasal surgery, or want to protect your from new injury, like wearing a mouth-guard when wrestling, using a custom-fitting face mask is the only effective means of ensuring prevention of costly injuries that will also take you out of the game.
